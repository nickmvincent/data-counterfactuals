## Start broad, then follow the thread you need

Each collection names one corner of the larger picture. The point is not to force everything into one taxonomy; it is to make the overlaps easier to notice once you keep the training-data intervention itself in view.

The memo is trying to give a more formal account of how these problem framings connect. These shelves are a companion map: a way to move laterally from one literature to another once the shared structure is in focus.

Priority labels simply mark especially useful starting points for a collection. They do not mean the other papers are secondary in any global sense.
